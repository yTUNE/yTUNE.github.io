ABOUT US

Kape at Tinapay

Kape at Tinapay are located in San Manuel, Tarlac. Apart from cakes, they also offer high-quality pastries that are famous for their exotic combinations of ingredients. They also have a beautifully designed cafe.

Kape at Tinapay was founded in 2020 by Timothy Nicole Panis. The bread is baked with all-natural ingredients and given at least 24 hours to rest before they bake it twice in the steam oven.

Kape at Tinapay is a gluten-free bakery in San Manuel, Tarlac. They make the purest gluten-free bread and pastries. The majority of the ingredients used are 100-percent organic, and everything that comes out of their ovens is made without gums, soy, and refined sugars.

Kape at Tinapay

Kape at Tinapay started as a small, independent neighborhood bakery in San Manuel,Tarlac. They make everything they can by hand. Hard work, high-quality ingredients, and an artisanal spirit combine to create the delicious treats that have made Kape at Tinapay famous. Kape at Tinapay has been involved with food charities and local communities for 2 years. Their most recent initiative is The Strawberry Cake.
